Anti Counterfeiting Solution

February 16, 2021 • Posted by Mukti Sharma

What is Counterfeiting / Intellectual Property (IP) Theft?

Counterfeiting is a federal and state crime involving the manufacturing or distribution of goods under someone else’s name and without their permission. Counterfeit goods are generally made from lower quality components, in an attempt to sell a cheap imitation of similar goods produced by brands consumers know and trust.

This chart shows the percentage of total value of seized counterfeit and pirated goods worldwide in 2016, by industry.

The continuous growth of the global counterfeiting industry is a major cause for concern. Fueled by the proliferation of internet use and social media platforms, the magnitude of global physical counterfeiting is estimated to have increased considerably since the beginning of this century. ASPA has claimed counterfeit products across various sectors in India are causing losses to the tune of over Rs 1 lakh crore every year.

Water - believe it or not - is the most counterfeited product on earth.

Counterfeiting In India

In India, the Government has initiated steps to address counterfeiting, piracy, and smuggling. Two major efforts stand out. First, the legal framework is fairly well developed in India. Second, the government has taken steps to protect consumers’ health and safety from dangerous counterfeits through significant education efforts. Despite these actions, a study by FICCI shows that counterfeit, piracy, and smuggling rates remain high.

Our Solution

We at root2ai provide industries or companies with an anti-counterfeiting solution to deal with counterfeiting of their products. For this, we provide unique QR codes for every single product. Only a shop-owner can scan them also just for a single time, at the end of the chain,i.e., when a customer buys a product. No one can use that code to scan in between except for the shop-owner and distributor. Let me explain in detail. When a product comes from industry, before the distribution they have to generate the QR codes in an amount equal to that of the number of products manufactured using our app. Now, they can add that QR code to the packaging of products. At the time of generation of QR code, there was an option of pin code which must be known to the QR generator and, to shop-owner only. Then, if someone else tries to scan the QR, they have to enter the pin code which is unknown to them. So, they will not be able to scan the QR. If anyone will try to rescan that code by making a copy of the QR, it will prompt fake product as a message on the screen.

Industrialists can also monitor how much sale their particular batch has made after the correct scan of QR. Also, they get areas of trade. It helps industrialists to monitor where their product is popular and where there is low salability. So, they can work on the advertisement for the products in the specific areas of a city. It will save time, efforts, and money of those industrialists or companies.
